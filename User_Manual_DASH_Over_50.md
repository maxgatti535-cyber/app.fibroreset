# DASH Over 50 Method: User Guide & Manual
**Version 2.1 – US Edition**

Welcome to the **DASH Over 50 Method**, your personal health companion designed to simplify cardiovascular wellness through the Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension (DASH) plan. This guide will help you navigate the application and get the most out of your AI Coach.

## 1. Getting Started
### Profile Setup
Upon your first launch, you will be guided through the **Profile Setup**. Providing accurate information about your age, weight, and existing medical conditions helps the AI Coach tailor its advice specifically to your needs. 

*   **Tip:** You can update your profile or change measurement units (US/Metric) at any time in the **Settings** menu.

### Your 7-Day Trial
The application includes a 7-day free trial that begins as soon as you complete your profile. During this time, you have full access to all premium features, including the AI Coach.

---

## 2. Using the AI Coach
The AI Coach is the "brain" of the application. It is available 24/7 to answer your questions and provide support.

### How to Interact
*   Type your question in the chat box or use the **Microphone** icon to speak directly to the Coach.
*   Use **Quick Actions** for common tasks like "3-Day Plan" or "Daily Check-in."
*   **BP Context:** Tap "Include recent Blood Pressure readings" before asking a question to give the Coach up-to-date information about your trends.

### Example Questions
*   *"What are some low-sodium alternatives to soy sauce?"*
*   *"Can you suggest a beginner exercise for high blood pressure?"*
*   *"I'm eating out at an Italian restaurant; what should I order?"*

---

## 3. Health & Lifestyle Tracking
### Blood Pressure Log
Track your systolic and diastolic readings daily. The app automatically categorizes your readings (e.g., "In Range," "High," or "Crisis Level") to help you understand your trends.

### Medication Tracker
List your daily medications and dosages. Check them off each day to ensure you never miss a dose.

### Meal Plans & Education
Access the **Meal Plan** and **Education** sections to discover DASH-friendly recipes and learn the science behind sodium management. These sections are integrated with the AI Coach for deeper explanations.

---

## 4. Progress & Insights
The **Progress** tab provides a visual trend of your blood pressure readings and medication adherence over time. Use these charts during your next doctor's visit to show your lifestyle changes.
