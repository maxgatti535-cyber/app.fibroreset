📱 How to Install the App

This application is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You don't need to visit an App Store. You can install it directly from your browser to your home screen.

On iPhone (iOS)

1. Open Safari and navigate to the app link.
2. Tap the Share button (the square with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen.
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4. Tap Add in the top right corner.

The app icon will now appear on your iPhone home screen like a regular app.

On Android

1. Open Chrome and navigate to the app link.
2. Tap the Menu icon (three dots) in the top right corner.
3. Tap "Install App" or "Add to Home screen".
4. Confirm by tapping Install.

🚀 How to Use the App

1. First Time Setup

When you open the app for the first time, you will be asked to complete your Profile. Please enter accurate information (age, weight, height, activity level) as this helps the AI Coach provide personalized advice.

2. Main Features

📊 Dashboard

Your daily command center. See a quick snapshot of your progress, upcoming reminders, and quick links to key actions.

🤖 AI Coach

Your 24/7 personal assistant. Ask questions like:

  • "What can I eat for a low-sodium breakfast?"
  • "Is this blood pressure reading normal?"
  • "Give me a motivation tip for today."

Note: The Coach uses your profile data to give tailored answers.

🍽️ Meals

Plan your weekly DASH-friendly meals.

  • Weekly Plan: View breakfast, lunch, snack, and dinner suggestions.
  • Shopping List: Automatically generate a list of ingredients based on your plan.
  • Recipes: Click on any meal to see full ingredients and instructions.

🏃 Exercises

Access gentle, age-appropriate workout routines.

  • Choose your level: Beginner, Intermediate, or Expert.
  • Use the built-in Stopwatch/Timer for your sessions.
  • Click "Ask Coach about these exercises" for safety tips or modifications.

❤️ Blood Pressure (BP)

Log your daily readings. The app tracks your history to help you see trends over time.

3. Settings & Privacy

You can update your profile or reset your app data at any time in the Settings menu. Your data is stored locally on your device for privacy.
